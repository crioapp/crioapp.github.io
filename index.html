About CRIO

CRIO is the assistant you always wanted when you needed digital marketing content for your brand quickly.

It provides you with beautiful and effective graphical content without needing any prior knowledge of designing and marketing.

How it works?

Content Creation

By asking few simple questions, CRIO understands your brand's identity, and your content's target audiences and goals. You can also add your message and visuals before CRIO uses all this information to create the most beautiful and effective graphical content for your brand.

Content Targeting

From industry research and testing data, CRIO finds the best suited designs for you. It gathers the contents, according to your audiences' likings from font size or colour combinations to symmetry or design style.
